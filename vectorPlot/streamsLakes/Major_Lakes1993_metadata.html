Abstract:
This layer contains lakes and wide streams selected from 1:100,000 scale US Census Bureau TIGER files. The featues were selected to approximately match data shown in USGS 1:2,000,000 scale DLG files.

In this layer, polygons may represent lakes or wide streams. These layers are intended for cartographic representation of lakes and streams rather than geographic analyses.

Feature names are mostly from the TIGER files. Some were obtained from from Montana DOT county maps and USGS topographic maps.

Process step:
Convert TIGER files to Arc/Info format and project them from Geographic coordinates to Albers. Performed at the Billings office of GeoResearch (406-248-6771).
Process date: 10/12/1991
Process step:
Select major streams and lakes from the TIGER data. This was performed between 10/1/92 and 7/23/93.
Process date: 07/23/1993
Process step:
Parrot Lake was incorrectly named Ester Lake. Corrected the name of Parrot Lake and added Ester Lake from TIGER data.
Process date: 02/10/2009
