Abstract:
This layer contains streams and lake shorelines selected from 1:100,000 scale US Census Bureau TIGER files. The featues were selected to approximately match data shown in USGS 1:2,000,000 scale DLG files.

In this layer, lines may represent the centerline of a stream, the shorelines of a wide stream represented by a polygon in the lakes layer, the shorelines of a lake, or a boundary between two polygons with different names.

Feature names are mostly from the TIGER files. Some were obtained from from Montana DOT county maps and USGS topographic maps.

Data Quality Information:
Attribute accuracy report:
Size classification is arbitrary. In some cases, one fork of a stream is used to represent the upper reaches of a stream, so the name may not be exactly accurate.

Completeness report:
This is an arbitrary selection of streams from the TIGER data. All known rivers were selected. Lakes and other streams were selected based on area or length, spacing, and familiarity.


Horizontal Positional Accuracy Report:
Approximately 50 meters, assuming the source maps meet National Map Accuracy Standards
